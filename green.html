Alternative to grass

  • Thyme: No watering needed. Blooms for about a month (good for insects). Then you can walk on it. Some types can be eaten and used for tea
  • Carpet vervain: feels similar to lawn, robust plant that regenerates quickly. Small pretty blossoms. Downside: top part dies over winter & grows back
  • Plumage (cotula dioica). Few blossoms (good if you worry about stepping on insects)
